IDEAHS is a research group at the University of Santiago de Compostela that views innovation, research, and the value of arts and humanities not merely as an academic container, but as something that can also be shared and given back to society.

Drawing on two core axes — research and outreach — and inspired by the name itself, we built the identity around the concept of the idea: that moment of discovery and the desire to share it with others, symbolised as "Idea!" or, in academic tradition, "Eureka!"
