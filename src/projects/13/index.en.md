Consibertromics is a project managed by the Acuigen group at the University of Santiago de Compostela and the Genetics Ichthyology Laboratory at the University of Girona. Its goal is to improve the conservation of native brown trout populations on the Iberian Peninsula through a genomic approach.

The website serves as a documentary repository to record and disseminate information and advances related to the research.
