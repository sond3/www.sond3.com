Graphic design for the 25th anniversary of the declaration of the Lugo Wall as a World Heritage Site, held at the Castro Museum in Viladonga and the University Museum Domus do Mitreo.

The project takes stone as the central element: the granite present in the wall becomes a symbol of permanence, time, and collective construction. A graphic image is built that dialogues with the idea of structure, resistance, and historical memory.
